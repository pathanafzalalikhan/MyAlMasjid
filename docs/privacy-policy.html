Privacy Policy

Effective: 3 April 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how My AlMasjid ("we", "our", "us") collects, uses, stores, and shares your personal data when you use our mobile application ("App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with applicable laws including India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023 (DPDPA) and the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

1. Data We Collect

We do not collect emails, financial information, health data, contacts, racial/ethnic origin, sexual orientation, or political/religious beliefs.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Authenticate your account (phone OTP)
  • Display your profile within your masjid community
  • Send prayer-time and announcement notifications
  • Calculate Qibla direction (on-device, data never leaves your phone)
  • Find masjids near your location
  • Monitor app stability and fix crashes (Crashlytics)
  • Serve contextual advertisements (Google AdMob)

4. Data Retention

  • Account data (name, phone, DOB, photo) — retained as long as your account is active.
  • Prayer notifications — dispatch records deleted automatically after 30 days.
  • Crash logs — retained for 90 days by Firebase Crashlytics.
  • Live Azan audio — never stored; ephemeral only.
  • On account deletion all personal data is permanently erased within 30 days.

5. Your Rights (DPDPA 2023 & GDPR)

As a user you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction — update inaccurate data via your in-app profile settings.
  • Erasure / Account Deletion — delete your account from Profile → Delete Account. All linked personal data is permanently erased within 30 days.
  • Withdraw Consent — disable notifications at any time in app Settings or your device notification settings.
  • Grievance Redressal (DPDPA) — raise a complaint with our Data Protection Officer at privacy@myalmasjid.com. We will respond within 30 days.

6. Children's Privacy

My AlMasjid is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with their data, please contact us immediately at privacy@myalmasjid.com.

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard safeguards including:

  • Sensitive credentials stored in Android Keystore / iOS Keychain (never in plain storage)
  • All data transmitted over HTTPS (TLS 1.2+)
  • Firebase App Check (Play Integrity) — prevents unauthorised API access
  • Rate limiting and input validation on all API endpoints

8. Advertising (Google AdMob)

We display ads via Google AdMob. Google may use your device identifier and approximate location to serve contextual or personalised ads depending on your consent. EU/EEA/UK users are shown our GDPR consent form on first launch (powered by Google's User Messaging Platform). You can opt out of personalised ads at any time via Settings → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation on your Android device.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Effective" date at the top. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy. Significant changes will be notified via an in-app banner.
