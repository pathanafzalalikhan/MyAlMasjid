Privacy Policy — MyAlMasjid
Last updated: March 5, 2026

MyAlMasjid ("we", "our", "the app") is operated by the developer.

INFORMATION WE COLLECT
- Account information: name and email address when you register
- Prayer tracking data: your personal prayer check-in history (stored on your device and our servers)
- Device information: device type and OS version for app compatibility
- Push notification token: to deliver Azan alerts and announcements

HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION
- To provide prayer times and Azan notifications
- To display community announcements from your Masjid admin
- To store your prayer tracking history
- We do not sell your data to any third parties

DATA STORAGE
Your data is stored securely on our servers (Railway cloud platform) and protected with Firebase Authentication.

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES
- Firebase (Google) — authentication and push notifications
- MongoDB — database storage
